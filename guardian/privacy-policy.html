Privacy Policy

Application: Guardian

Package: com.guardian.moldova

Version: 1.0.0

Effective date: Upon Google Play release

Introduction

Guardian is a protection application for elderly users and their families in the Republic of Moldova. It helps identify suspicious phone calls and dangerous fraud situations in real time.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Guardian uses, why it is needed, and how it is handled.

Key principle: Guardian processes information directly on your device. It does not require an account, does not send personal data to external servers, and does not share data with third parties.

Information Guardian Uses

Information Purpose Stored
Call state Determine when a phone call is active so protection can operate. No
Incoming phone number Determine whether the caller is familiar. No
Call duration Help identify unusually long calls that may require additional attention. Memory only
Contacts (lookup only) Reduce false alerts for trusted contacts. No
Call log (lookup only) Determine whether a caller is familiar. No
App usage statistics Help identify situations that may require additional attention. No
Accessibility status Protection readiness verification. No
Installed package events Help identify newly installed applications. No
Protection readiness Remember application configuration. Local device only
Language preference Remember the selected language. Local device only

Guardian Does Not

  • Read SMS messages.
  • Record phone calls.
  • Record audio.
  • Track your location.
  • Access your camera.
  • Create a user account.
  • Send personal data to external servers.
  • Share your information with third parties.

Permissions Used

Permission Purpose
READ_PHONE_STATE Determine when a phone call is active.
READ_CALL_LOG Help determine whether a caller is familiar.
READ_CONTACTS Reduce false alerts for trusted contacts.
PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS Help identify situations that may require additional attention.
FOREGROUND_SERVICE Maintain continuous protection.
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED Restart protection after device reboot.

Data Storage

Guardian processes information locally on your device. It does not use cloud storage or backend servers.

  • Temporary protection information exists only in memory.
  • Application settings are stored locally in SharedPreferences.

Data Sharing

Guardian does not share personal information with third parties. The application contains no advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or cloud processing services.

Data Deletion

  • Temporary information disappears when the application closes.
  • Local settings can be removed by clearing application storage or uninstalling Guardian.

Children

Guardian is intended for general users, including older adults. It is not specifically directed toward children under 13.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated as Guardian evolves.
